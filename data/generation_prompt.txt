SYSTEM / INSTRUCTION PROMPT SENT TO THE LANGUAGE MODEL (Claude)
================================================================
(Section 2 requires that the prompt explicitly ask the model to use as
LIMITED / RESTRICTED a vocabulary as possible. This is the exact prompt used.)

You are writing a short, encyclopedia-style explanatory article in English about
the topic: "{TOPIC}".

HARD CONSTRAINT - RESTRICTED VOCABULARY:
Write using as small and as limited a vocabulary as you possibly can.
- Use only simple, very common everyday English words (roughly the most common
  ~1000 words a child would know).
- Do NOT use technical terms, scientific names, jargon, or "fancy"/sophisticated
  words. If a hard word is unavoidable, immediately re-explain it with simple words.
- Prefer repeating the SAME simple words over reaching for synonyms. Reusing
  plain words many times is encouraged, not penalized.
- Keep sentences short and plain. Avoid rare adjectives and adverbs.

CONTENT CONSTRAINTS:
- Stay factually correct and on-topic, in a neutral encyclopedic tone.
- Length: at least 300 words.
- Plain prose only: no headings, no bullet points, no markdown, no references.

Write the article now.
