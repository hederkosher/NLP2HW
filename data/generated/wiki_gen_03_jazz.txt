Jazz is a kind of music. It started in the United States a long time ago, more than one hundred years ago. Black Americans made this music first, and it grew out of older kinds of music that they played and sang. Over time, jazz became known and loved all over the world. Today, people in many countries play jazz and listen to it.

One big thing about jazz is that the people who play it often make up the music as they go. This means they do not always play the same notes each time. A player may start with a known song, but then he or she will change it and add new notes right there while playing. This is called making it up on the spot. Two players can play the same song and make it sound very different. This is one reason why jazz is so much fun to hear.

Jazz uses many kinds of instruments. Some players use horns that they blow into. Some play the piano. Some play drums to keep the beat. Some play a big string instrument that they pluck with their hands. Often a group of players will play together. While one player makes up a part, the others play behind to hold the song together.

Jazz can sound many ways. Some jazz is fast and full of life, and it makes people want to move and dance. Some jazz is slow and soft, and it can sound sad or sweet. Because players can change the music so much, there are many kinds of jazz.

Many people think jazz is one of the great gifts that the United States gave to the world. It has changed a lot over the years, and it still grows and changes today. People still love to play it and hear it, and it is taught to young people in many schools all over the world.
