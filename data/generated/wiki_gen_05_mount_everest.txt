Mount Everest is a mountain. It is the highest mountain on Earth. This means that the top of Mount Everest is higher than the top of any other mountain in the world. The mountain is so high that its top reaches very far up into the sky. Mount Everest is found in Asia, in a long line of tall mountains. It sits between two countries, and people from both countries live near it.

Because the mountain is so high, the top is a very hard place to be. It is very, very cold there, even in the warm part of the year. There is also a lot of snow and ice on the mountain, and strong winds blow near the top. High up on the mountain, the air is thin, which means there is not much air to breathe. This makes it hard for people to walk and to think clearly. For all these reasons, the mountain can be a very dangerous place.

Many people from all over the world want to climb to the top of Mount Everest. To climb it, they must train hard and bring warm clothes, food, and other things they need. The climb takes many days, and people must stop often to rest and to let their bodies get used to the thin air. Some climbers ask local people who know the mountain well to help them and to carry things.

Climbing the mountain is not safe. Over the years, some people who tried to climb it have died on the way. The cold, the thin air, and the snow and ice all make it very risky. Still, each year many people try, because reaching the top is a great dream for them.

People first reached the top of Mount Everest many years ago. Since then, many other people have climbed it and have stood on the highest place on all of Earth.
